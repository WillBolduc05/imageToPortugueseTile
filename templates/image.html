Welcome to MyPortugueseTile!

MyPortugueseTile is a simple service designed to convert any image into a traditional portuguese-style tile mural.
(For those who are unaware, this means a tile mural using a blue-tan color scheme with a pencil sketch-like style.)
You can upload any image in jpeg, jpg, or png format, and your output will be returned as a jpeg file.
This is a free service and we hope you enjoy!
Documentation and the process used to convert the image can be found here: https://github.com/WillBolduc05/imageToPencilSketch
